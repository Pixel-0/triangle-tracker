Get your MATH on!!

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. Triangle-tracker is an application that enables you to identify the type of your triangle. You will input its values, submit and get results.

A few notes on triangles

  • Equilateral: all sides are equal.
  • Isosceles: only two sides are equal.
  • Salene: all sides are unequal.

Enter the dimensions of your triangle to determine it's type.
